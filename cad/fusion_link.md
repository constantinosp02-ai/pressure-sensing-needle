# Fusion 360 model

The full assembly is committed in this folder as both a Fusion 360 archive and neutral exports.

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Whole Assembly.f3z | Fusion 360 archive of the full assembly. Open with File, Import, Fusion 360 archive. |
| Whole Assembly.step | STEP export for cross-software use (SolidWorks, Inventor, FreeCAD, etc.). |
| Pressure Sensing Needle Set-up.stl | STL mesh for FDM printing the syringe stand. |

The model is described in Section 4.5.1 of the report. The PDF render of the assembly is Figure 11.
